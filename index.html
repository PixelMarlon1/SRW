Privacy Policy

Spaceship Rescue

Information We Collect

Game Center Integration:Spaceship Rescue integrates with Apple's Game Center to provide you with the option to report your high scores to leaderboards. When you choose to use this feature, Game Center may collect and store the following information:

  • Game Center Player ID: A unique identifier assigned to your Game Center account.
  • High Scores: Information about your in-game high scores, achievements, and rankings on leaderboards.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected via Game Center solely for the purpose of reporting and displaying high scores on leaderboards. We do not use this information for any other purposes, such as marketing or third-party advertising.

Opt-In Choice

Participation in the Game Center leaderboard is entirely optional. You can choose whether to enable or disable this feature within the game settings. If you opt-in to use the leaderboard feature, your high scores will be reported to Game Center and displayed on leaderboards. If you opt-out, your high scores will not be shared.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and have implemented appropriate measures to protect it. Your Game Center information is stored securely within the Game Center service, and we do not have direct access to your Game Center account credentials.

Third-Party Services

Please note that while using Game Center, you may be subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We recommend reviewing Apple's policies for a comprehensive understanding of how they handle your data.

Children's Privacy

Spaceship Rescue is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with their personal information without parental consent, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove the information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we collect and protect your data.

Contact Us
